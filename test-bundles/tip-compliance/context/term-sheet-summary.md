## 2. Key Provisions

### Liquidation Preference

- **Type**: Non-participating preferred
- **Multiple**: 1x
- **Seniority**: Series B ranks senior to Series A and Seed in liquidation waterfall
- **Conversion**: Series B Preferred converts to Common Stock at any time at the holder's election, at a 1:1 conversion ratio (subject to anti-dilution adjustments)

### Anti-Dilution Protection

- **Type**: Broad-based weighted average
- **Trigger**: Issuance of equity below the Series B price per share ($8.00), excluding standard carve-outs (employee options, strategic partnerships approved by the Board, etc.)

### Dividends

- **Type**: Non-cumulative
- **Rate**: 6% of the Original Purchase Price per annum
- **Payment**: Payable only when and if declared by the Board of Directors
- **Preference**: Series B dividends are paid prior to any dividends on Common Stock or junior preferred stock

### Voting Rights

- **Basis**: On an as-converted basis (1 share of Series B Preferred = 1 vote)
- **Protective Provisions**: Standard protective provisions requiring Series B majority consent for:
  - Changes to the Certificate of Incorporation that adversely affect Series B
  - Authorization of any senior or pari passu security
  - Any merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets
  - Redemption or repurchase of any equity (excluding standard employee repurchase)
  - Incurrence of debt exceeding $2,000,000
  - Increase in the size of the Board of Directors beyond the agreed composition
  - Any transaction with a related party exceeding $250,000

## 6. Investor Rights

### Information Rights

- Monthly financial statements (unaudited), due within 30 days of month end
- Quarterly financial statements (unaudited), due within 45 days of quarter end
- Annual financial statements (audited), due within 120 days of fiscal year end
- Annual budget and operating plan, due within 30 days of fiscal year start
- Cap table updates upon any equity transaction

### Registration Rights

- **Demand registration**: Series B investors may demand up to two registrations after the earlier of (a) 5 years from closing or (b) 180 days after the company's IPO
- **Piggyback registration**: Standard piggyback rights on any company-initiated registration
- **S-3 registration**: Unlimited S-3 registrations once the company is S-3 eligible

### Pro-Rata Rights

All investors holding at least 500,000 shares of preferred stock shall have the right to participate pro-rata in future equity financing rounds.

### Right of First Refusal and Co-Sale

- **ROFR**: Company and investors have the right of first refusal on any proposed transfer of founder shares
- **Co-sale**: Investors have co-sale rights on any proposed transfer of founder shares exceeding 1% of outstanding shares
